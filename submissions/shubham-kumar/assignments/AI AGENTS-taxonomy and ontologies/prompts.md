## Prompt 1 — Framework Understanding

> Explain LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen from first principles. Focus on where each one breaks, not just what it does. Include OpenClaw/NemoClaw/ZeroClaw as deployment-focused variants.

## Prompt 2 — Taxonomy

> Build a multi-dimensional taxonomy of agentic AI systems covering: coordination style, autonomy level (L1-L5), and deployment context including physical/embodied AI. What dimension is the industry ignoring?

## Prompt 3 — Ontology

> Define a complete ontology for multi-agent AI systems. Map all relationships: agent to memory, tools, environment, other agents, audit layer, and governance policy. Which relationships are missing from current frameworks?

## Prompt 4 — Physical AI Gap

> What changes when you deploy agentic AI in physical systems vs digital environments? How should taxonomy and ontology frameworks evolve to account for embodied AI, sensor noise, and real-world consequence severity?

## Prompt 5 — Cybersecurity Dimension

> What are the cybersecurity vulnerabilities specific to multi-agent systems? Cover prompt injection, agent hijacking, inter-agent communication attacks, and audit trail manipulation.
