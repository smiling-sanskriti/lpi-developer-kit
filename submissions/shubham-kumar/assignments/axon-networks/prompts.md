# AXON Networks Research Prompts

## Prompt 1

> Research AXON Networks the telecom company (not Axon Enterprise). Explain their actual product architecture — NEURA, digital twin, OaaS. How does their multi-agent system differ from frameworks like LangChain or AutoGen?

## Prompt 2

> What are the political and geopolitical implications of AXON Networks' partnership with Cassava Technologies for pan-African telecom infrastructure? Consider AI sovereignty, critical infrastructure control, and emerging market dynamics.

## Prompt 3

> What cybersecurity vulnerabilities exist in a closed-loop multi-agent AI system that makes autonomous decisions on live broadband infrastructure? Consider agent hijacking, digital twin breach, and multi-tenant attack surfaces.

## Prompt 4

> Compare AXON NEURA's domain-specific agent approach vs generic frameworks like LangChain and AutoGen. What are the architectural tradeoffs? When should you build proprietary vs use open-source agent frameworks?
